# Home Exercises
*Pilates and home exercises prescribed across training sessions*
*RAD Advanced Foundation — Exam: May/June 2027*

---

### Private Lesson — 22 June 2026
| Exercise | Notes | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Bird dog — one arm to fifth, second, by the thigh, back to tabletop | Core and back strength. Keep pelvis stable throughout | Home |
| Plank with rocking weight forward and back | Full body stability. Don't let hips drop or rise | Home |
| Cobra / seal / back extension | Keep head and neck in line with the body throughout — don't crank the neck | Home |
| Glute engagement pressing up into upper back extension, then arm to low arabesque | Engages lats and shoulder blades. Shoulder blades pinned down and back throughout | Home |

### Private Lesson — 23 May 2026
| Exercise | Notes | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Side plank | Hips level, don't collapse forward. Left side active not passive | Home + class prep |
| Side plank with arm to fifth | Shoulder blade stays down as arm rises. Obliques initiate, not shoulder | Home |
| Clamshell with hips lifted | Pelvis stable, don't rock back as leg opens | Home |
| Lying shoulder lift with twist to opposite hip | Oblique initiates before shoulder moves — this is the épaulement feel | Home |
| Slow Russian twists with ball, straight arms | Ribcage rotates, shoulders down, no traps at end range | Home |
| Ball pass around the back | Shoulder blades stay down throughout, don't round the upper back to reach | Home |
