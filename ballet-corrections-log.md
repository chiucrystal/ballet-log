## Syllabus Corrections

### AF-10: Centre practice and pirouettes
- Double pirouette en dehors: axis collapses before rotation completes — closing in 3rd not 5th. Affects **control** and **dynamic values**
- Double pirouette en dehors: arm-driven rotation rather than hip-initiated. Affects **co-ordination**
- Pirouette preparation: weight not fully forward in fondu — limited ankle bend restricting depth. Affects **correct posture and weight placement**

### AF-11F: Pirouette enchaînement
- Same pirouette axis and preparation issues as AF-10 apply here
- Lame duck (posés pirouettes en dehors): compact fourth under the body, not fifth. Affects **co-ordination**
- Two double pirouettes in enchaînement — axis pattern needs to be resolved before this exercise is performance-ready

### AF-12: Adage
- Arabesque: second shoulder hiking — outer hip and side body not stabilising torso. Affects **line**
- Arabesque: eyeline down toward floor. Affects **line** and **expression**
- Fouetté en l'air: deep hip rotators releasing too early before arabesque extension. Affects **control** and **dynamic values**
- Arms need continuous movement through adage for fluidity. Affects **co-ordination** and **dynamic values**

### AF-25: Variation 2
- Poses: posterior weight bias, not getting over the box. Affects **correct posture and weight placement**
- Poses: retiré not getting up fast enough. Affects **dynamic values**
- Pas de bourrées: traveling rather than turning on the spot — retiré not at full height. Affects **control**
- Eyeline: up and over, not throwing the head back. Affects **line** and **expression**
- Épaulement: more upper back twist needed. Affects **co-ordination** and **expression**
- Outer ankle and foot not firing fast enough before weight transfer to pointe. Affects **control** and **dynamic values**
